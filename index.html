Rules of the Game
The game consists of 2 players and 5 dice. The player with the highest score after 5 rounds wins.

1. Each round starts with the player rolling 5 unstuck dice.

2. Once the dice are rolled, those dice with numbers 1, 3, 4 or 6 get added to your round score.

3. Those dice that land on a 2 or 5 are 'Stuck' and removed from the unstuck total.

4. The active player continues to roll until all 5 unstuck dice are lost and the turn is over. The round score is then added to the player's total.

5. Player 2 repeats this process and so on until all 5 rounds are played out. At this point the player with the highest score wins !
